About Me

I'm a curious soul with a deep love for expression — whether it's through words, art, or ideas. I believe every emotion is a story waiting to be told.

When I'm not working on technical projects, I immerse myself in writing poetry, capturing fleeting thoughts and feelings. My paintings often reflect the beauty and chaos of my inner world.

I dream of a future where creativity and technology meet — building tools that empower others to express themselves. My goal is to live a meaningful life driven by connection, honesty, and inspiration.

I love long walks, old notebooks, Bengali songs, abstract sketches, and moments of deep reflection. This space is my way of archiving who I am beyond code.
